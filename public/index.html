🛠️ How It Works
A technical overview of the CryptoSpike ecosystem and signal lifecycle.
1
Advanced Signal Generation
The CryptoSpike backend core continuously scans 200+ USDT pairs on Binance. It identifies high-probability setups using a combination of technical indicators (Volatility, Momentum, and Volume spikes) and multi-timeframe analysis.
2
AI Confidence Scoring
Every signal is pre-evaluated by our proprietary AI model. It assigns a **Confidence Score (0-100)** to help you prioritize trades. The score factors in Bitcoin's dominance (Market Bias) and the specific pair's historical success rate within the system.
3
Live WebSocket Monitoring
The Dashboard acts as your **Live Monitoring Terminal**. It establishes a direct WebSocket connection to Binance exchange to provide real-time, sub-second P&L updates for every active signal, ensuring you never miss a price move.
4
Automated Lifecycle Management
The signal lifecycle is fully automated. The backend monitors your trades 24/7, automatically triggering **TP1, TP2, or TP3** hits. After reaching a target, the system activates a **Trailing Stop Loss (TSL)** to protect your locked-in profits from market reversals.
5
Intelligent Insight Analysis
The learning loop completes in the Insights Hub. Our AI Signal Analyst (Llama 3.3) analyzes your unique historical data to suggest optimizations, such as better score thresholds or specific timeframes that yield higher win rates.
6
Profit & Statistics Calculation
Profit is calculated using standard market formulas: (Exit - Entry) / Entry * 100 for Longs and (Entry - Exit) / Entry * 100 for Shorts. The Total Realized Profit on your dashboard is the cumulative sum of these percentages from all closed signals.
🚀 User Success Guide
Follow these best practices to maximize your trading consistency with CryptoSpike.
  • Prioritize High Scores: Focus on signals with a Confidence Score > 80. Historically, these have a significantly higher hit rate for TP2 and TP3.
  • Check Market Bias: Before entering a trade, look at the Market Bias bar in the Insights tab. It's safer to go Long when the bias is >60% Bullish.
  • Let the TSL Work: The Trailing Stop Loss is calibrated for volatility. Avoid manual exits; let the system lock in profits automatically as the price moves.
  • Daily AI Scan: Use the Analyze Now button every morning. The AI identifies temporary anomalies in the market that static charts might miss.
  • Manage Your Risk: Use the Win Rate by Score table to adjust your position size. If a score bracket has <50% WR, reduce your exposure.

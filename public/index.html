📚 Dashboard Guide
A comprehensive guide to interpreting CryptoSpike signal statuses and performance metrics.
Active Signals (Live)
⌛ WAITING : The signal has been initiated but has not yet reached any designated targets. Profit/Loss fluctuates in real-time.
🔄 TP1 / TP2 (Running) : The signal has successfully reached Target 1 or 2. It remains dynamically tracked as it progresses towards the maximum target.
Closed Signals (History)
🏆 TP3 (Closed) : The final target has been achieved. The signal is successfully concluded with maximum profit.
❌ SL HIT : The price hit the initial Stop Loss before reaching any targets. The signal is concluded with a loss.
🛡️ TSL HIT : Trailing Stop Loss. The signal initially reached a profitable target (TP1/TP2) but subsequently reversed and hit the ascending protective stop. The position is closed at a breakeven or partial profit.
📊 Profit & Loss Tracking
Total Performance (Realized P&L): Located at the very top of the dashboard. This represents your actual, finalized profit. It exclusively and permanently records the final percentage sum from all closed historical trades (TP3, SL, or TSL).
Net Unrealized P&L (Floating): Located under live signals. This represents temporary "paper" profit. It dynamically tracks the real-time profit of all currently active signals, which may fluctuate until the signal is formally closed.
